BIO

Duran Duran is a British "new wave" Band formed in the city of Birmingham, England, in 1978 by keyboardist Nick Rhodes and bassist John Taylor, with the later addition of drummer Roger Taylor, guitarist Andy Taylor (no, none of the Taylors are related) and lead singer Simon Le Bon. Duran Duran has been a band for over 40 years and has been known as the "Fab 5", in what is sometimes refered to as the MTV-driven 'Second British Invasion' of the 1980's. Sadly, Andy Taylor left the group in 2006, but the other four didn't miss a beat and their positive-vibe music remains just as relavant and enjoyable today as it was with the release of "Rio" in 1982... have a listen to some new stuff!
